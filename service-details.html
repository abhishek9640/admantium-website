What is Real-World Asset Tokenization?

Real-world asset Tokenization is the process of converting tangible and intangible assets into blockchain-based digital tokens. Real-world asset Tokenization allows fractional ownership, global accessibility, and enhanced liquidity for traditionally illiquid assets.

  • The global asset tokenization market is projected to reach $5 trillion by 2030, with real estate, precious metals, and equities leading to wider market adoption.
  • Studies indicate a CAGR of 22% for asset tokenization, this shows increasing opportunities across industries.
  • In 2024, the total value locked in tokenized assets reached over $176 billion, marking a 32% increase year-to-date, with non-stablecoin assets growing by 53%.
  • Enhanced Liquidity: RWA Tokenization increases market participation and provides liquidity to traditionally illiquid assets by converting assets into digital tokens. Fractional ownership makes it easier to buy and sell portions of assets.
  • Alternative Funding Mechanisms: Tokenization enables businesses to raise capital by issuing digital tokens representing asset ownership or revenue streams. This approach can attract a broader investor base and provide more flexible funding options compared to traditional financing methods.

Benefits of Real-World Asset Tokenization

Enhanced Liquidity

Traditionally, selling illiquid assets could be a lengthy and expensive process. Asset tokenization unlocks a global marketplace for these assets, enabling faster and cost-efficient transactions. Imagine selling a portion of your commercial property or a rare collectible to a wider pool of interested buyers instantly.

Fractional Ownership

Tokenization allows to raise capital by selling fractions of your assets, attracting a broader investor base, and unlocking new funding opportunities. This is beneficial for financing illiquid assets like private equity or real estate developments.

Improved Asset Management

Tokenization streamlines asset management by automating tasks associated with ownership records and dividend distribution. This reduces administrative burdens and frees up resources for asset owners.

Our Tokenization Services

Real Estate Tokenization

Real estate tokenization involves converting property ownership into digital tokens. This enables fractional ownership and simplifies the investment process.

Benefits

Increased Liquidity: Facilitates easier buying and selling of property shares.

Fractional Ownership: Lowers the investment barrier, allowing more investors to participate.

Global Reach: Attracts a broader investor base beyond geographical limitations.

How its work?

We digitize property ownership by issuing blockchain-based tokens representing fractional shares of the tokenized property. This will allow investors to buy and sell smaller portions of real estate assets globally, bypassing traditional barriers.

Precious Metals Tokenization

Tokenized precious metals like gold and silver provide increased liquidity, security, and 24/7 market participation.

Benefits

Enhanced Liquidity: Enables quick and efficient trading of precious metal assets.

Security: Utilizes blockchain technology to ensure transparency and reduce fraud.

Accessibility: Allows investors to engage in the precious metals market without physical storage concerns.

How its work?

Physical precious metals like gold and silver are securely stored, then equivalent digital tokens are issued. These tokens can be traded in the global market or redeemed for the underlying asset, offering the next level of asset liquidity.

Precious Stones Tokenization

Tokenized precious stones such as diamonds enable fractional ownership and easier transferability, opening new investment avenues.

Benefits

Market Accessibility: Broadens access to high-value assets for a wider range of investors.

Transparency: Provides clear records of ownership and provenance.

Liquidity: Transforms traditionally illiquid assets into tradable digital tokens.

How its work?

High-value gemstones are authenticated, appraised, and converted into digital tokens. This enables traceable ownership and broadens access to global markets.

Equity Tokenization

The equity tokenization process digitizes company shares and streamlines the capital-raising process while enhancing shareholder management using blockchain technology.

Benefits

Efficiency: Simplifies the issuance and management of shares.

Liquidity: Enables secondary market trading of equity tokens.

Transparency: Improves corporate governance through immutable records.

How its work?

Shares of a company are digitized into tokens that can be distributed to investors. This process democratizes access to equity and simplifies ownership transfers.

Debt Tokenization

Tokenized debt instruments like bonds and loans enhance their tradability and open new funding sources.

Benefits

Liquidity: Allows debt instruments to be traded more easily.

Transparency: Provides clear records of debt issuance and ownership.

Efficiency: Reduces administrative costs associated with debt management.

How its work?

Loans, bonds, or other debt instruments are tokenized, allowing lenders and investors to trade these assets on blockchain platforms. This enhances liquidity and investor reach for financial institutions.

Art & Collectibles Tokenization

Tokenized art and collectibles allow for fractional ownership, making high-value items more accessible to a broader audience.

Benefits

Accessibility: Enables more investors to own a share of valuable art pieces.

Liquidity: Facilitates easier buying and selling of shares in collectibles.

Security: Ensures provenance and authenticity through blockchain records.

How its work?

Unique art pieces and collectibles are digitally tokenized, providing opportunities for fractional ownership while maintaining the integrity of the original assets.
